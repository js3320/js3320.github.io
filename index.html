About Me

I am Jiho Shin, a final-year MEng Biomedical Engineering student at Imperial College London. My passion lies in developing data-driven tools that improve diagnosis and treatment in healthcare. Through independent projects and research internships, I’ve explored multimodal learning, reinforcement learning for oncology, and interpretable CNNs for dermatology.

Beyond technical depth, I value collaboration and mentorship — leading undergraduate teams, teaching first-year students, and contributing to AI community initiatives. I approach research with curiosity, structure, and persistence, always aiming to translate algorithms into clinically meaningful insights.

Currently, I’m preparing for PhD study in AI-driven healthcare, focusing on medical imaging, multimodal data fusion, and reinforcement learning for personalised treatment.
